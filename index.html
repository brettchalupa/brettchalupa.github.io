Hi, my name is Brett Chalupa.

I build software for the web.

I am a human being living in Burlington, Vermont. I try to live a simple life. I enjoy reading, writing, music, cooking, and walking.

I build software for clients. I work on the front-end and back-end of web applications, from the words in the app to the APIs that power it. I believe in the web, open technologies, and free/libre software. I appreciate simple design, and I am capable of envisioning and implementing my own designs.

I enjoy programming with Ruby, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. Ruby on Rails and Ember.js are the two frameworks that get me excited about building web applications. I use Inkscape for design. I use Git for version control.
