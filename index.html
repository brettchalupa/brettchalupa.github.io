Hi, my name is Brett Chalupa.

I build software for the web.

I help folks plan, design, and build software. I care about sustainable code, user experience, and building the the right thing.

I work on the front-end and back-end of web applications, from the words in the app to the APIs that power it. I enjoy programming with Ruby, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. I believe in the web, open technologies, and free/libre software. I appreciate simple design, and I am capable of envisioning and implementing my own designs.

I live in Burlington, Vermont. I live a simple life. I enjoy reading, writing, music, cooking, and walking.
