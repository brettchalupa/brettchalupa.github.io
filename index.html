Hi, my name is Brett Chalupa.

I build software for the web.

Who I Am

I am a human being living in Burlington, Vermont. I try to live a simple life. I enjoy reading, writing, music, cooking, and walking.

I currently work at FullStack, where I build software for clients. I work on the front-end and back-end of web applications, from the words in the app to the APIs that power it. I believe in the web and open technologies. I appreciate simple design, and I am capable of envisioning and implementing my own.

I enjoy programming with Ruby, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. Ruby on Rails and Ember.js are the two frameworks that help me build things. I use Sketch for design, and I use Git for version control.

What I Have Done

I most recently worked on Supermarket, the community site for Chef. I previously worked at Burton Snowboards, and I work on freelance projects from time to time.

I occasionally write notes and work on open-source projects.
